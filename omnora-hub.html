📚 Build Better Study Habits

  • Study a little every day.
  • Practice instead of memorizing only.
  • Ask questions whenever you don't understand.
  • Review previous lessons regularly.
  • Take short breaks while studying.
  • Stay consistent instead of studying only before exams.

⚠️ Things Every Student Should Avoid

  • Procrastination.
  • Exam malpractice.
  • Copying assignments without learning.
  • Too much time on social media.
  • Negative peer pressure.
  • Giving up after small failures.
